Synopsys Microelectronics Olympiad 2016 in Armenia

Earn a chance to represent the Philippines on the final stage of the Olympiad in Armenia! 
 

Details

Your school is invited to join the 11th Annual International Microelectronics Olympiad of Armenia. The first stage, which is a short multiple-choice exam, will be held online at your respective universities on Friday, August 26, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM. This will be done simultaneously with other universities in the Philippines. The top scorer will be sent to Armenia to represent our country for the final stage of the Microelectronics Olympiad.

  

Topics

The topics to be covered by the Olympiad are:

  • Digital IC Design and Test
  • Analog and Mixed Signal IC Design and Test
  • Semiconductor Devices and Technology
  • Mathematical and Algorithmic Issues of EDA 

For more details, please visit the Microelectronics Olympiad website.
